Campus & Facilities

Our school is located just 1 km from mudbi and is surrounded by lush greenery and beautiful gardens. The campus includes modern classrooms, science labs, computer labs, a library, sports grounds, and transport facilities.

Academics

We offer a diverse curriculum including Sanskrit, Hindi, English, Mathematics, Science, and Social Studies. Teachers ensure student-centered learning, focusing on building discipline, confidence, and inquisitiveness among students.

Extracurricular Activities

Students participate in music, dance, drama, painting, and sports. These activities ensure their holistic development and enhance creativity and teamwork.

Visitors & Recognition

Many dignitaries including Union Minister Bhagwanta Khuba and former CM Basavaraj Bommai have visited our campus and praised the trust's efforts in spreading quality education in rural areas.

Our Vision

Inspired by Lord Shiva, our goal is to provide meaningful education that fosters self-discipline, independent thinking, and a spirit of inquiry. We nurture students from all backgrounds to become thoughtful, responsible citizens of the world.
